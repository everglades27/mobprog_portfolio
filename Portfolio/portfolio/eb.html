Jasaan National High School

I attended and graduated my secondary education in a public secondary school that is very known as Jasaan National Highschool. The school was kinda crowded because there is a lot of students that study there, and some of them are already a friend of mine and a known person even their a year or two ahead of my age. The school is so fun actually and I met a lot of friends I didn't expect to have. Just like any other schools, they have particular events and programs that others don't have.
